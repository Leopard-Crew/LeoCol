Evidence and Provenance

Evidence describes where LeoCol found system provenance information.

Evidence sources may include LaunchAgents, LaunchDaemons, Login Items, StartupItems, kernel extensions, CUPS queues, and receipt/BOM records.

Resolved

Resolved means LeoCol connected the observation to an existing source, file, path, or record.

Observed only

Observed only means LeoCol saw a reference but did not resolve it to a current filesystem object or complete source.

Evidence is descriptive. It is not a repair recommendation.
